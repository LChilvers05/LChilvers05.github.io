Work Experience

Mar 2020 - Sep 2021: Mobile Application Developer for Dicam Technology Ltd

Dicam Technology Ltd provides microprocessor-based control solutions for farmers. I was put in charge of the design, development and deployment of Dicam Charts, an app which presents historical data of a farm's parameters for customers to monitor. I also worked on updates to the main settings control app, Dicam 2, which included an alarm notification system, Bluetooth communication, user experience improvements as well as significant bug fixes. This job has taught me how to build software as part of a team with a scrum methodology to meet the requirements of the company and its stakeholders, skills which I have brought to university by having a role of leadership in group work. Working through the coronavirus pandemic, I communicated solutions to my colleagues remotely and in office and when one left the company, wrote documentation for their work so other team members could easily understand. It has consolidated my learning while studying at university, an example being my work with SQLite for graph data point storage which strengthened my grade in my Databases module.


2017-2020 - Drum Tutor
